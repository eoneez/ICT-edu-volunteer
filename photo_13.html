 Hidden stories about this picture







This is a picture of me going out to Gyeongbokgung Palace
in Seoul wearing Hanbok, a traditional Korean costume, last fall.
At that time, the palace was allowed to be viewed at night for
a limited time, and Gyeongbokgung Palace, which was revealed as
a lighting, was really cool. I think it was more beautiful because
there were many people who went out wearing hanbok. If you come to
Seoul, I recommend you to wear Hanbok and visit Gyeongbokgung Palace. It will be remembered a lot.
